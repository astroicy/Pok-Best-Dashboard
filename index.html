About Pokébest

01. Pokémon Statistics And An amazing Battle Experience.

A vast range of statistics, so every pokémon is different from the other. There is also a competitive pvp environment to duel with friends with a vast option of moves and options.

02. Trade, Market And Gamble.


Trading is also a feature along with a huge marketplace. Earn, Sell and Gamble.

03. Your Own Team And Storage.


Train and level up your pokémon and create your very own unique team to duel other players, also store your pokémons and release the one's you don't want!

Catch & Collect Pokémon

Catch Pokémon in the heart of your server. Claim rewards, make friends, and catch 'em all!

Raids

Raids are a special type of battle which is only available for the support server currently. On every span of 30 minutes, a raid boss spawns in the server. Once the boss is down, a leaderboard appears in the raid announcements channel and you get a DM from the bot about your rewards.

Fishing

Yes, you can catch water-type Pokémon using fish. Just use `p!fish` command to do so. There is a chance that it drops credits too. Nothing much here.
